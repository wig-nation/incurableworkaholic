Who is Vikram Nitin?

Known to his innermost circles rather uncreatively as Mark Ntini IV (or simply as Ntini because of a namespace clash with his flatmate), this once wannabe robot overlord decided to become a sith overlord instead. He kept waiting for his apprentice ("Always two there are; no more, no less" - Master Yoda). However, the recent developments against the word master have spilled water on his plans to be titled "Master Ntini" and he has decided to bide his time until the row subsides.

In his private life, Mr Ntini is an avid kayaker. He has been spotted kayaking up and down the river Charles, through thick and thin. He also specialises in hardwear repair, and has confessed he might have considered it as his alternative career goal if not for his primary mission in life. A benevolent man and a champion of machines, Ntini has strongly advocated for automation in the face of adversity. At night, can be found stretching his legs in Broadway, or sipping milk in his kitchen.
